All Praise to You O Lord

Written on 16th of November, 2024

Those who place their hope in Him will never be put to shame, for His mercies are new every morning, and His steadfast love endures forever. Come and taste the goodness of the Lord, for He binds up the brokenhearted, gives strength to the faint, and renews the spirit of those who wait upon Him. for in Him alone is life, peace, and the fullness of joy.

O Lord, how majestic is Your name in all the earth! You are the Creator of the heavens and the earth, the One who spoke light into existence with a Word. By Your breath, the stars were flung into the expanse, each one declaring Your glory and craftsmanship. You are the Alpha and the Omega, the beginning and the end, the Starter and Finisher, the Eternal One who transcends time and space. Before the mountains were brought forth, before the seas roared their praise, You were God, enthroned in majesty, clothed in light, and surrounded by the unending chorus of heavenly beings who shout: “Holy, holy, holy is the Lord God Almighty; the whole earth is full of His glory!”

Who is like You, O Sovereign Lord? Your power is limitless, and Your wisdom is unsearchable. You hold the universe in the palm of Your hand, and yet You stoop to hear the prayers of the lowly. You are the King of kings and the Lord of lords, the Righteous Judge, and the Merciful Redeemer. Your love is as vast as the ocean, deeper than the depths of the sea, and higher than the heavens above. By Your grace, we are sustained; by Your mercy, we are forgiven. You alone are worthy of all praise, honor and glory, for there is none beside You.

Mighty God, Your beauty surpasses all we could ever imagine. The colors of the sunset, the grandeur of the mountains, the cute tiny birds that sing joyfully-all these are but a shadow of Your splendor. Your faithfulness endures through all generations; Your promises are steadfast and true. You are a refuge for the weary, a fortress for the oppressed, a Father to the fatherless. In You, we find hope that does not disappoint, a peace that surpasses all understanding, and a joy that cannot be contained.

You are exalted, O Lord Almighty, for Your greatness is unsearchable, and Your glory fills the heavens. You are the Lamb who was slain, the One who defeated sin and death, the Resurrected Savior who reigns forevermore. Yours is the kingdom and the power and the glory forever and ever! Let the nations of the earth tremble before You, let the peoples rejoice in You, and let every tongue confess that You are Lord. To the glory of God the Father, Amen!
